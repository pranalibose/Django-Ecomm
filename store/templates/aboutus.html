About Us


  • BOSS Leathers was established in the year 1994.
  • Over the last more than two decades it has emerged as one of the finest companies with regard to best quality Shantiniketan Leather products (Leather handicrafts) as its speciality.
  • Of late the brand has expanded into pure leather (tanned) products which have gained immediate popularity due to its highest quality.
  • The brand deals in premium products and have been able to create a niche for itself and is now expanding in the canvas and jute products also.
  • We have been working with and supplying to all major handicrafts promoting showrooms and major export houses.

Our Vision...

To be the number one Leather Handicrafts Products company through continuous innovation, consistent quality and becoming the second skin of our customers.


Our Mission...

To serve people globally and become the most sought after brand by building TRUST.
